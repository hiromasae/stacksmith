## Visual & Design Rules (hard constraints)

These are non-negotiable. Do not deviate without explicit approval.

- **Font:** Inter throughout. No display-font swaps for headings or anything else.
- **Color:** Functional blue is reserved for interactive elements only (links, buttons, active states). Text is near-black. Borders are light gray. Cards are white.
- **No gradients.** No shadows heavier than a subtle border.
- **Cards:** Defined by a border, not a shadow. Tight radius. Information-dense.
- **Banned:** No purple, no indigo, nothing that reads as a generic AI product.
